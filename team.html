Judge

To apply to judge e-mail: joinus@ethberlin.com.
please note that judges cannot be mentors or hackers as well.
Please apply for judging ONLY if you don’t want to participate on the aforementioned two categories. There is room for everyone.
Write us an e-mail with the subject “So, I think I can JUDGE!!!” and why this is interesting for you.

Judging process is designed and executed by Chris Heymann, Lasse Claussen, and Mareen Gläske


We expect all volunteers, mentors and judges to accept and adhere to our Code of Conduct.
